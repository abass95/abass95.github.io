About Us




Professional Experience

My name is Anthony Basciano and I’ve become a successful mortgage agent with Pegasus Mortgage and Financial Solutions throughout the years by focussing on customer service and client satisfaction. I aim to provide all of my clients with the highest quality of service possible by being honest, reliable, and by always putting the needs of my clients above anything else. I always look forward to finding the absolute best mortgage solutions that address the specific needs of my clients and use my expertise to make sure deals close on time. At Pegasus Mortgage and Financial Solutions, I am effectively supported by a team of directors, brokers, underwriters and administrators who truly believe in putting the client first and strive to provide fast and efficient service. This teamwork and professionalism has helped me build long- term relationships within the industry as well as my growing client base.

Pegasus Mortgage and Financial Solutions

Pegasus Mortgage and Financial Solutions opened its doors in August of 2008. It began as a one-man brokerage but has grown substantially since that time, with the current Pegasus team comprising over 100 mortgage agents, specialists and administrators. Pegasus is now one of the most trusted names in the business. A big reason for this growth is due to their business philosophy which is centred around providing the best service possible. Pegasus realizes that clients are the core of their business and as such they will do as much as possible to keep their clients happy. Customer service is of utmost importance. Along with their great reputation for providing phenomenal service, Pegasus has also grown substantially due to their success rate with clients. Pegasus simply gets deals done! They accomplish this by putting together a fantastic team of agents, specialists, and administrators who are the best and the brightest in the industry. In addition to having a wealth of knowledge that they are eager to pass down to their clients, the Pegasus team is extremely collaborative and there is constant support in making sure deals close, and customers are happy. Throughout this astounding growth Pegasus Mortgage and Financial Solutions has been able to forge many important relationships with financial lenders and business partners, and these relationships have enabled their clients to receive some of the best mortgage products on the market. With their business philosophy focussing on unparalleled service, results, building important business relationships, and assembling an exceptional team, Pegasus Mortgage and Financial Solutions has forged its way to the top of the mortgage industry.
